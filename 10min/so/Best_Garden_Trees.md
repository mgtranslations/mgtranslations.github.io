## Xididdada Geedaha Ma Waxay Weeraraan Tuubooyinka Musqusha?

- Dooro geedo yar haddii aad ka baqayso khilaaf la xididdada iyo tuubooyinka musqusha.
- Geedaha waaweyn waxay leeyihiin xididdo fidsan; geedaha leh xididdo ‘xoogan’ waxaa ka mid ah:
  - Willow, Ash, Poplar, Cottonwood, Sweet Gum, Sycamore, qaar Maple ah (Norway, Silver, Boxelder, Red).
- Xididdadu waxay aadayaan halka ay ka helaan biyo iyo hawo, waxayna geli karaan dildilaaca ama meelaha daciifka ah ee tuubooyinka.
- Xaddidaadaha laamiga waxay sababaan in xididdadu kor ama hoos u galaan.

**Kheyraad:**  
[Hagaajinta Deegaanka iyo Adeegyada: Dhibaatooyinka, Ka Hortagga, iyo Xulashada Dhirta](https://www.extension.uidaho.edu/publishing/pdf/CIS/CIS0991.pdf)

## Dhibaatooyinka Fiilooyinka & Dhismayaasha Marka Geedka La Beerinayo

- **Buugga Milkiilaha Geedka ee USDA**: [www.treeownersmanual.info](https://www.treeownersmanual.info)
  - Geedka ka fogee ugu yaraan 3 feet laamiga, 15 feet dhismaha iyo geedaha kale.
  - Haddii geedku korayo in ka badan 30 feet, ka fogee 25 feet fiilooyinka korontada sare.
  - Geedaha dhaadheer ee gaara 60 feet ama ka badan waa in la dhigo ugu yaraan 35 feet ka fog guriga si xididdadu u koraan si fiican.
- **Ururka Caalamiga ee Arboriculture**: [www.isa-arbor.com](https://www.isa-arbor.com)
  - Geedaha lagu beero 15 feet gudahood ee fiilooyinka korontada waa inaysan dhaafin 20 feet marka ay weynaadaan.

## Arrimaha Carrada & Biyaha ee Geedka La Beerinayo

- Xididdadu waxay u baahan yihiin hawo iyo biyo labadaba.
- Carrada caafimaadka leh waxay u oggolaataa hawo iyo biyo inay dhex maraan; carrada la adkeeyay sidaas ma aha.
- Geedka dhig meel aanay xididdadu hoos kaga qarsanayn laami ama xabag.
- Hel il biyo ah oo si fudud loo heli karo.
- Biiro aagga xididdada inta lagu jiro xilliga degaanka (3-da sano ee hore).
- Geedo qaarkood waxay u baahan yihiin biyo xilliga abaaraha.
- Cawska iyo geedka marar badan ma wadaagaan baahida biyo.
- Geedo qaar, sida cypress iyo oak-da deegaanka, ma jecla biyo badan xagaaga.

## Iibashada Geedka: Weyn ≠ Degdegsiimo Badan

- Kobaca caadiga ah wuxuu bilaabmaa marka geedka la beddelay uu helo isku dheelitirka laamaha iyo xididdada ee la mid ah geed aan la beddelin.
- Geedaha yar ee la beddelay way dhaqso badan yihiin inay koraan.
- Geedaha waaweyn waxay u baahan yihiin waqti dheer si ay u koraan.
- Ka dib marka la beddelo, geeduhu si tartiib ah bay u koraan sanado badan, xilligaas oo xididdadu si tartiib ah u gaarayaan cabbirka geedka aan la beddelin.

**Kheyraad:**  
[Saamaynta Cabbirka Geedka ee Kordhinta iyo Koritaanka Ka Dib Marka la Beero](https://journals.ashs.org/horttech/view/journals/horttech/15/1/article-p118.xml)

## Talooyin Beerista

### Ha U Beerin Si Aad Ugu Xumaato

- Hubi in xididdada sare (root flare) ay ka sarreyso heerka ciidda.

### Geedka Xidho Kaliya Haddii loo Baahan Yahay

- Haddii geedku aad u culus yahay ama goobta ay dabaylo badan yihiin.
- Geli labo tiir oo adag dhulka ka hor intaadan beerin si aanay xididdadu u dhaawacmin.
- Xadhigga dhig hoose (saddex-meelood meel jirridda hoose), laastik u reeb si geedku u dhaqaaqo.
- Isticmaal agab aan dhaawici doonin jirridda.
- Ha xidhnayn wax ka badan hal sano.

### Biyo Si Wanaagsan U Si

- Biyo badan sii haddii aad beerayso xilli kulul.
- Xilliga dayrta biyaha dheeraadka ah lagama baahna.
- Geedka biyo sii ka hor inta uusan barafku dhicin.
- Geedaha evergreen-ka ah biyaha way isticmaalaan xitaa xilliga qaboobaha.
- Biiro aagga xididdada inta lagu jiro xilliga degaanka (3-da sano ee hore).
- Todobaadkii, 2 gallon halkii inch ee jirridda.
- Xididdadu waxay fidiyaan meel ka ballaaran laamaha sare.
- Biyo si tartiib ah ugu shub aagga xididdada:
  - Khadka dhirta / tuubada biyaha si tartiib ah OR
  - Daloolo hoose ee baaldi 5-gallon ah, dhig aagga xididdada, kadibna buuxi.

### Isticmaal Mulch

- 4 inch oo ah qoryaha la jajabiyey ee beeraleyda dusha ciidda (cilmi baaris ayaa sheegtay in xitaa 12 inch la isticmaali karo).
- Qoryaha cusub ayaa ka fiican kuwa la qudhmay—waxay quudiyaan fangaska waxayna kobciyaan noolaha ciidda.
- Ha ku darin ciidda dhexdeeda.
- Dabool aagga xididdada oo dhan, si tartiib ah ugu dhaaf jirridda.

**Kheyraadka:**

- [Qoryaha Bilaashka ah ee la Jajabiyey](https://portlandgeneral.com/outages-safety/safety/tree-maintenance/free-wood-chips)
- [Isticmaalka Qoryaha Beeraleyda Sida Mulch Beerta](https://pubs.extension.wsu.edu/search?q=FS160E)
