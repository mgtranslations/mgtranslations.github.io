## Ku Saabsan Great Plant Picks

Great Plant Picks (GPP) waa barnaamijka waxbarasho ee ugu weyn ee Beerta Botanical Elisabeth C. Miller. 2001, liiska ugu horeeya ayaa la daabacay iyadoo talooyin laga bixinayo dhisidda xulasho dhir tayo leh oo loogu talagalay gobolka xeebaha Pacific Northwest. Ilaa hadda, in ka badan 1,000 oo dhir aad u wanaagsan ayaa loo doortay beeralayda deggan galbeedka Buuraha Cascade laga bilaabo Eugene, Oregon, USA ilaa Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada.

### Xeerarka Xulashada

- Waxay ku kori karaan noocyo kala duwan oo carrada ah.
- Uma baahna biyo badan oo dheeraad ah.
- Waa u adkaysi kulaylka.
- U adkaysi qabowga USDA zone 7.
- Kuma badna cudurro ama cayayaan dhibaato leh.
- Waxay keenaan xiiso in ka badan hal xilli beerta.
- Dhirta GPP badankoodu waxay leeyihiin faa’iidooyin deegaan (kororso shinnida, cunto shimbiro, hoy xayawaan, iwm).
