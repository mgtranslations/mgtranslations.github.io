# Ubaxa Ugu Fiican Shinnida --- Cilmi-baadhista Beerista [cite: 1]

Liiskan wuxuu ka koobanyahay dhirta uu Dr. Gordon Frankie, Jaamacadda California, iyo asxaabtiisu caddeeyeen inay yihiin kuwa ugu fiican ee soo jiidata shinnida [cite: 1]. Dhirta asal ahaan ka soo jeeda Oregon ee soo jiidata shinnida ayaa ku salaysan cilmi-baadhis uu sameeyay Aaron Anderson, Oregon State University Garden Ecology Lab [cite: 2]. Isha: California Bees & Blooms ee Frankie iyo asxaabtiisa, blogs.oregonstate.edu/gardenecologylab/ [cite: 3].
